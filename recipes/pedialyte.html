Pedialyte

Did you drink the other two recipes in the same night?!

This will get you back on your feet in no time.

Ingredients

  • Pedialyte

Steps

  1. Get a regular glass and fill it with ice. If this step is overly reminiscient of your martini or negroni, skip.
  2. Pour pedialyte into said glass.
  3. Top off with a bit of water.
  4. Repeat this loop until you can take that sleep mask off for 10+ minutes.
