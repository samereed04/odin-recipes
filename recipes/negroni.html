The Negroni

The bitter cocktail for when you're feeling sweet.

Classic cocktail for the gin drinker. Wear it with your bathing suit or tuxedo.

Ingredients

  • Gin
  • Sweet vermouth
  • Campari

Steps

  1. Put some rocks in a glass
  2. Grab your bottle of Campari. Hold it over your glass. Flip it upside down and count to two.
  3. Repeat step two with the sweet vermouth.
  4. Repeat step three with the gin, but count to three this time.
  5. Rub an orange peel around the rim and drop it in the glass.
  6. Repeat for all of your friends.
