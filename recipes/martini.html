The Martini

How did we convince ourselves that this is a cocktail?

A (preferably cold) glass of gin. Gin!

Ingredients

  • Gin
  • Vermouth if you have it
  • Olive juice if that's your thing

Steps

  1. Put some rocks in a shaker
  2. Dump a bunch of gin in said shaker.
  3. If you like olive juice, add some of that.
  4. Vermust you add anything else?
  5. Enjoy.
